Preface


Create a self-devised or collaboratively developed work that responds to the concepts, processes, and practices explored in the class while taking the artistic agency to experiment on its own terms. The work may draw upon or extend the midway project or be something entirely new. How can the work be experienced, performed, exhibited or screened within the affordances of our online environment? Collectively, we will conceive of the final as both an event and an archive, creating a designed interface to house the works (or portions of them) as urls. While each piece should have a link accessible within the archive, the work can also expand beyond this link. For example, the url might be activated by a live reading, performance, or other intervention (that might also be recorded and integrated into archive). A glitch url makes the most immediate sense for the class but other approaches should be possible on an individual basis. Event and critiqued on December 9. Each project will have 30 minutes for presentation and feedback.
