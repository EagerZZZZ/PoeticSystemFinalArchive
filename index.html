Preface


Create a self-devised or collaboratively developed work that responds to the concepts, processes, and practices of our shared context of poetic systems while taking the artistic agency to experiment on its own terms. The work may draw upon or extend an earlier project or be something entirely new. How can the work be experienced, performed, exhibited or screened within the affordances of our online environment? We will conceive of our culmination as both an event and an archive, creating a programmed interface to house the works which may also expand beyond their links, activated by live readings, performances, or other interventions.
